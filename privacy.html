🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: July 1, 2025

✓ Short version: GoldSignals AI does not collect, sell, or share personal data from any TikTok users. We only use the TikTok API to publish content to our own account. No viewer information is ever accessed or stored.

1. Overview

GoldSignals AI ("we", "our", "the app") is an automated video publishing system. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in connection with our application and use of the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal data of any TikTok viewer or user
  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
  • TikTok user profiles, followers, or account details
  • Comments, likes, shares, or any engagement data from TikTok
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data of viewers
  • Any sensitive personal or financial data

3. Data We Handle (Our Own)

The only data our system processes belongs to us — not to any external user:

4. TikTok API Usage

We use the TikTok Content Posting API exclusively to upload video content to our own TikTok account (@goldsignals_ai). Specifically:

  • We only request the video.publish OAuth scope
  • We do not request access to any user's personal account data
  • We do not read, copy, or store any TikTok platform user data
  • API tokens are stored encrypted on our private server and never exposed

5. TikTok Login (OAuth)

We use TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow solely to authenticate our own business account. We do not implement a "Login with TikTok" feature for end users. No third-party user ever logs in through our application.

6. No Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data with third parties. We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or data brokers. The only external services we interact with are:

  • Telegram — to receive signals from our own operated channel
  • TikTok API — to publish videos to our account
  • TTS Service — to generate audio voiceovers (no personal data sent)

7. Security

OAuth tokens and API credentials are stored in an encrypted secrets vault on our private server. Access is restricted to authorized operators only. No credentials are ever logged or exposed in video content.

8. Children's Privacy

Our trading signal content is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect or process data from minors under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our published content after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
